ABOUT BTS

BTS is a south korean boy group formed by 7 members, it debuted the 13 of june of 2013 under Big it Entretaiment with the song "No More Dream", with their debut song they win the new artist of the year award on Melon Music Awards, Golden Disc Awards 2013 and in Seoul Music Awards 2014, but they started to win influence with their album "Dark & Wild", "The Most Beautiful Moment in Life, part 2" and "The Most Beautiful Moment in Life: Young Forever"
